# Cold Outreach Pipeline for B2B SaaS — Research Project

## Topic
Cold Outreach Pipeline for B2B SaaS

## Why I Chose This Topic
Cold outreach is the highest-leverage GTM channel for early-stage B2B SaaS.
Unlike paid ads or SEO, it produces pipeline within days, requires no budget,
and forces clarity on ICP and messaging. I chose this because it sits at the
intersection of copywriting, systems thinking, and data.

## Expert Selection Rationale
I specifically avoided bloggers and content writers.
Every expert here either runs an active outreach agency,
hosts a podcast with real campaign data, or has built
a software product in this space.
The goal was signal over volume.
