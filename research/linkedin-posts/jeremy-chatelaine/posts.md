## Post 1
**Date:** 2025
**URL:** https://www.linkedin.com/in/jeremychatelaine/

Why multichannel cold outreach books 4.22x more B2B meetings than email alone:

Most people treat cold email as the only outbound channel.
That is a mistake.

Here is what the data from QuickMail campaigns shows:

→ Email alone: baseline results
→ Email + LinkedIn combined: 4.22x more meetings booked
→ Multi-channel outreach combining email, LinkedIn, and phone:
   can boost results by over 287% vs email alone

The key insight: your prospect needs to see you in multiple places
before they feel comfortable enough to reply.

Email deliverability is also now the single biggest variable in campaign performance.
Before worrying about subject lines or copy — fix your infrastructure:

- Set up SPF, DKIM, and DMARC authentication (mandatory since Feb 2024)
- Keep spam complaint rate below 0.1%
- Warm up new sending domains before scaling volume
- Use inbox rotation to protect sender reputation

If your emails do not reach the inbox, nothing else matters.

**Source:** QuickMail internal campaign data, 2025

## Post 2
**Date:** 2025
**URL:** https://quickmail.com/authors/jeremy-chatelaine

Cold outreach is not dead. It just requires more intention than before.

Here is what the Cold Email Outreach Podcast (400+ episodes) has taught me:

→ Cold outreach starts working once your list, strategy, and message are aligned
→ The hardest lists to build often deliver the highest reply rates
→ Single channel focus vs multi-channel: go multi-channel as soon as possible
→ Preview text is just as critical as your subject line for open rates
→ Great agencies do not stop at outreach — they make sure clients see ROI

The 30/30/50 rule for cold email success:
- 30% depends on your content and copy
- 30% depends on your list quality
- 50% depends on your follow-up strategy

Most people obsess over copy and ignore list quality and follow-up.
That is why they fail.

**Source:** Cold Email Outreach Podcast with Jeremy Chatelaine and Jack Reamer
