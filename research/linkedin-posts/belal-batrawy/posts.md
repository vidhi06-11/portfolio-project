**Date:** 2024
**URL:** https://www.linkedin.com/posts/belbatrawy_sales-activity-7161485109966155776-j2a5

If you are cold calling in 2024 you need to use the Mic Drop Method.

I spent 15 years on the phones doing pure outbound at 5 startups to figure out
how to start more dialogues with buyers.

Here is the Mic Drop cold call framework:

-- Permission --
"Hey [First Name], this is Belal. I will be upfront, we have not spoken before.
Got 30 seconds for me before you throw your phone out the window?"

-- Problem --
Lead with the problem your prospect already knows they have.
Do NOT pitch your solution yet.

-- Provoke --
Pick on the thing that annoys them daily.
The tedious tasks they dread.
Really pick at that sore spot to snap them to attention.

-- Profit --
Show them how you eliminate their enemy and make their work life easier.

Key insight: Do not expect prospects to agree to a 30 minute meeting
based on a cold email no matter how good it is.
Instead propose a 5 minute chat.
If you get them interested in 5 minutes they will let you go on.

## Post 2
**Date:** 2024
**URL:** https://www.linkedin.com/in/belbatrawy

After 15 years and over $15 million generated doing outbound sales,
I have learned that the key is relentlessly targeting your prospect's "enemy."

Their inbox and calendar might be filled with noise,
but you need to cut through and grab their attention.

Forget leading with how you can solve their problems.
They are not even aware they have a problem yet.

Instead, pick on the stuff that annoys them daily.
The tedious tasks they dread.
Really pick at that sore spot to snap them to attention.

Then show them how you eliminate their enemy and make their work lives easier.

So do not just pitch your product.
Pick a fight with your prospect's status quo.
Show them how much better life can be.
