AEs, buckle up. 2024 will be an even heavier year of outbound for most of you.

We worked with Connor (an AE) and helped him land 3 new opps in one day from outbound.

Here's how he did it:
1) Prioritize 1 hour every day for outbound
2) Cut cold email copy down by half (from 150 words to 75 or less)
3) Impact over features (e.g. "better visibility" vs. "shorter sales cycles")
4) Work with SDRs vs. making them do the grunt work
5) Find 2-3 common personalization patterns and create templates
6) A/B test until you're blue in the face
7) Niche down the personas and industries into your top two
8) Stay persistent (12-15 total touches per prospect before stopping)

## Post 2
**Date:** 2024
**URL:** https://www.linkedin.com/posts/jasondbay_sales-prospecting-outbound-activity-7118566905648439296-DeRy

Is outbound a big priority or first-time focus for your sales org in 2024?
Don't make these mistakes:

⛔️ Letting marketing, enablement, or senior execs create the sequences
✅ Run a messaging workshop with your top AEs, SDRs, and CSMs

⛔️ Not providing training for front-line leaders to reinforce and coach outbound
✅ Train the front-line leaders before training the reps

⛔️ Front-line leaders don't participate in any cold outreach
✅ Front-line leaders get in the pit and make cold calls with reps

Bonus: Sales execs do this once or twice per quarter to rally the troops

⛔️ Not celebrating successes in Slack, all-hands calls, etc.
