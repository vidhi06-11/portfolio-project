**Date:** 2025

How I generated $20 million in B2B SaaS sales leads in one year:

The secret was spending 2 minutes personalizing each email
instead of blasting templates.

Custom cold emails convert at 7-8%.
Templates convert at 1%.

Here is the exact framework:

**Finding Qualified Leads:**
- Use BuiltWith to find companies by technology stack
- Use LinkedIn to identify decision makers
- Check job boards for buying signals
  (a company hiring designers = they need design tools)

**Writing the Email:**
- Spend 2 minutes on the first line — make it about THEM specifically
- Keep the body under 75 words
- Add a strong PS with social proof
- End with one simple yes/no question

**Following Up:**
- 5 to 7 touchpoints across multiple channels
- Follow-up emails every 2 days
- Connect on LinkedIn
- Comment on their blog posts or tweets

Most people give up after 1-2 emails.
The meeting is usually booked on touchpoint 4 or 5.

## Post 2
**Date:** 2025
**URL:** https://www.linkedin.com/in/alexanderberman/

Fix your cold email deliverability — this is not 2019.

Modern cold email requires proper infrastructure.
Here is how to do it right:

→ Use dedicated email sending systems — not Gmail or Outlook directly
→ Distribute sending across multiple domains
→ Keep volume low per inbox — maximum 20-30 emails per day per inbox
→ Warm up every new domain for at least 2 weeks before sending
→ Never add unsubscribe links to cold emails
  (that flags you as bulk mail — cold email should look like a 1-to-1 message)

The goal is not to churn and burn.
The goal is to deliver high quality targeted emails at scale
without triggering automated spam filters.

I have helped over 14,000 founders, agencies, and B2B teams
fix their cold email and scale to millions in revenue.

The infrastructure is always the first thing to fix.
Great copy on broken infrastructure = zero results.
