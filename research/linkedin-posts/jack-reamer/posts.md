# Jack Reamer — LinkedIn Posts

## Post 1
**Date:** 2025
**URL:** https://www.linkedin.com/in/jackreamer/

When you are building your cold outreach campaigns, ask yourself:
"What is the one specific pain point my prospect has that nobody else is addressing?"

Most campaigns fail not because of bad copy — but because of bad strategy.

The real drivers of a successful cold outreach campaign:
- List quality (are you reaching the right people?)
- Offer clarity (is your value prop immediately obvious?)
- Personalization depth (does this feel like it was written just for them?)

At SalesBread we have sent over 1 million LinkedIn messages.
Our average reply rate is 19.98%.
Our average qualified sales inquiry rate is 48%.

The difference between us and everyone else?
Ultra-personalized outreach. Every single message references something specific to that prospect.

Stop sending spray-and-pray. Start sending one-to-one.

## Post 2
**Date:** 2025
**URL:** https://podcasts.apple.com/us/podcast/cold-email-outreach-with-jeremy-jack/id1272643794

Key lessons from the Cold Email Outreach Podcast (co-hosted with Jeremy Chatelaine):

→ Keep your first message simple — your only goal is to get a reply, not make a sale
→ After scraping post engagements, enrich the data to filter by ICP criteria
→ At 25 connection requests per day you can land 100 new connections per week
   at a 70%+ acceptance rate without triggering LinkedIn throttling
→ The real focus should be converting connections you already have into calls
→ Tactics like scraping post engagements do not stay effective forever
   — the best time to test any new strategy is today

Most cold outreach problems are not solved by adding more personalization or tweaking copy.
They are solved by choosing the right people, with the right offer, and the right strategy.
