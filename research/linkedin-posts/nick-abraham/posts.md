11 sentences about the best cold emails: (Required reading for 2024)

The best cold emails:
1) Are under 50 words
2) Use an informal tone
3) Don't include attachments
4) Are optimized for scanning
5) Are completely value-driven
6) Are free of all industry jargon
7) Are personalized at least once
8) Use a soft or interest-based CTA
9) Are written at a 6th-grade reading level
10) Are only sent to people that can benefit
11) Have a subject line that looks like an internal email

In short:
→ Respect your prospect
→ Make it worth reading for them
→ Don't send something you wouldn't read

Cold email sequence 101: (must-know ahead of 2024)

Every email in the sequence needs to:
- Be well-targeted
- Be 2-3 sentences max
- Have a no-brainer offer
- Be personalized and relevant
- Have a soft CTA offering sales asset

Each sequence should be 4 steps (1 cold, 3 follow-up):
Email 1 - Offer + Value
Email 2 - Follow-up + Value/Sales Asset
Email 3 - Follow-up + Value/Sales Asset
Email 4 - Break-up

It'd be pretty hard not to see results with:
- The right cold emails
- The right sequence settings
- The right deliverability hygiene
