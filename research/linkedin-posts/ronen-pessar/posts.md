How I build trust with a stranger on a cold call:

There is one sure-fire way to build trust with a stranger — be 100% honest.

- No local-presence dialing (that is a trick and prospects know it)
- Be transparent and straightforward from the very first second
- Share how you actually feel

Examples of honest openers that work:
"I got your number from Apollo"
"Man, I feel like I sounded really salesy just now — can I try again?"

Trust is the currency of relationships.
And cold calling is the first step in a new relationship.
Start it with trust building — not tricks.

Most SDR teams are trained to use every hack available.
The reps who stand out in 2025 are the ones who lead with honesty.

## Post 2
**Date:** 2025
**URL:** https://www.demandbase.com/blog/top-b2b-sales-influencers-2025/

My two-phase system for building a high-performing outbound sales team:

**Phase 1 — Live Cold Call Pilot**
- Run a live cold call session in just 3 hours
- Target: achieve 20-40% connect rates in that session
- This proves the market is reachable before you invest in hiring

**Phase 2 — Recruit and Train In-House SDRs**
- Use the data from Phase 1 to build your hiring criteria
- Recruit SDRs within 90 days based on proven playbook
- Train them on the exact scripts and sequences that worked

Key principle: Quality conversations over vanity metrics.

Most B2B companies measure dial volume and email sends.
The only metric that matters is quality conversations with the right people.

A phone-first methodology still works in 2025 —
but only when combined with genuine research and honest outreach.
