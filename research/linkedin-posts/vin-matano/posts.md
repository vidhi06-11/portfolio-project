I received the best cold email I have ever gotten recently.

Here is why it worked:

1) There is personality in it — which AI usually sucks at
2) The AI knew I made TikToks and the subject matter of my content: preventing spammy sales tactics
3) The AI tied in personal interests throughout the entire email
4) It knows outbound best practices — the email is under 100 words and uses an interest-based CTA
5) It is not just personalized, it is relevant

The solution is solving a real pain for me — the time it takes to personalize emails.

I had to reply and ask "was this written by AI?"

The lesson: great cold email feels human, is short, and solves a specific pain.
